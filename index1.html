Welcome to Burnaby Palace

Burnaby Palace is a cornerstone in the Burnaby community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.

  • Authentic Chinese flavors with a modern twist
  • Fresh ingredients sourced daily
  • Expert chefs with decades of experience
  • Warm, inviting atmosphere
